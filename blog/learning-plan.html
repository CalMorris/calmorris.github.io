Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

  • My long term goal is to work as a full stack developer

  • Work on long term projects that people get use of everyday but have a lot of variety in day to day work.

  • Growth and challenge is very important to me. It’s easy when things become comfortable but it also gets stale quickly.

  • Work/life balance is important to me. I have previously had jobs where I felt there wasn’t any balance.

Strengths and limitations going into bootcamp

    Strengths:

    Persistent, hard working, attention to detail/perfectionist and curious.

    Weaknesses:

    I become fixated when something doesn’t work or I don’t understand something.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

  • Learn to work with many people on a project (ie pair programming). I am used to working on projects alone and this would is a skill I need to develop.

What non-technical skills would you like to develop while at Dev Academy?

  • Become a better communicator with both listening and speaking.

  • Learn to work with new people who and build rapport.

How will you manage yourself to work productively?

  • Create and maintain a routine of starting at 8:30am each day.

  • Begin each day by outlining goals and start with small tasks to build momentum.

  • Take regular breaks and reflect on progress.

  • Check in with peers and stay connected – will help keep me connected and motivated.

Describe what you expect from the Bootcamp team

  • Challenge my thinking and problem solving to become a better programmer.

  • Honest feedback that helps my growth. Even if it's an inconvenient truth.

Describe what you expect from yourself during Bootcamp

  • 100% commitment

  • Accept and be open to feedback. Help other people when I see they need it.

  • Make the smalls things big.
