Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

Work on long term projects that people get use of everyday but have a lot of variety in day to day work.

Growth and challenge is very important to me. It’s easy when things become comfortable but it also gets stale quickly.

Work/life balance is important to me. I have previously had jobs where I felt there wasn’t any balance.

Describe your strengths and limitations in relation to learning

Strengths are: Persistent, hard working, attention to detail/perfectionist and curious.

Weaknesses are: I become fixated when something doesn’t work or I don’t understand something.

What non-technical skills would you like to develop while at Dev Academy?

Become a better listener.

Become more present in conversions.

How will you manage yourself to work productively?

Create and maintain a routine of starting at 8:30am each day.

Begin each day by outlining goals and start with small tasks to build momentum.

Take regular breaks and reflect on progress.

Check in with peers and stay connected – will help keep me connected and motivated.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you?

Reflect on progress.

Make sure that I understand the problem.

Break the problem down into small bite sized chunks.

If I am stuck on a problem I will do the following:

Google solutions other people have used and understand why they worked.

Read documentation to make sure I understand the methods I am trying to use.

Reach out to cohort to see if anyone else has had a similar problem.

Post the obstacle problem on stack overflow to ask help from the community.

Describe what you expect from the facilitation team

Challenge my thinking and problem solving to become a better programmer.

Provide guided learning when stuck on a problem.

Honest feedback that helps my growth. Even if it's an inconvenient truth.

How will you schedule information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things?

Review week in advance and if there are any potential conflicts, look to compromise or discuss.
